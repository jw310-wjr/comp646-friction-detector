CVPR-style progress report
============================

Files:
  ProgressReport_cvprformat.tex   (main; matches course template preamble)
  egbib.bib                       (references)

You MUST copy from your COMP course / CVPR author kit into this docs/ folder:
  cvpr.sty
  ieee.bst                        (if not already pulled in by cvpr.sty)

Optional: create docs/figures/ and add PDFs referenced in the .tex (or keep fbox placeholders).

Compile (from this docs/ directory):
  pdflatex ProgressReport_cvprformat
  bibtex ProgressReport_cvprformat
  pdflatex ProgressReport_cvprformat
  pdflatex ProgressReport_cvprformat

Overleaf: upload cvpr.sty, ieee.bst, egbib.bib, ProgressReport_cvprformat.tex, and figure PDFs.
